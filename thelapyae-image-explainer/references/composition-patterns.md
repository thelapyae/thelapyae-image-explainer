# Composition Patterns

Choose one pattern per image:

- Workflow: input, avatar-operated transformation, output.
- Before and after: disorder on the left, changed state on the right.
- Bottleneck: the avatar repairs, widens, or reroutes a blocked physical path.
- Sorting: the avatar separates mixed items by purpose or state.
- Layered method: the avatar assembles a small stack of irregular modules.
- Route: the avatar carries or connects an idea across a sparse path.
- System fragment: show only three to five meaningful parts.
- Mini comic: two or three moments showing a state change.

## Invent A Metaphor

1. Convert the abstract idea into a physical verb such as filter, untangle,
   compress, bridge, catch, weigh, repair, route, or recycle.
2. Select one low-tech object such as a drawer, pipe, pulley, box, scale, gate,
   ladder, workbench, sieve, spool, or strange hand-built machine.
3. Make the avatar perform the verb using that object.
4. Remove anything that does not support the central idea.

Do not reuse old scenes by default. Change both the main object and the avatar's
action when illustrating a similar concept again.
